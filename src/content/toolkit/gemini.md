## Where it helps

- **Productivity**: Draft emails, summarize long documents, or plan your week in a 5-minute conversation
- **Writing**: Get a first draft for anything — messages, reports, presentations, or even difficult replies you've been putting off
- **Learning**: Ask Gemini to teach you something new, one concept at a time, using examples from topics you already enjoy
- **Research**: Upload a PDF or paste a long article and ask for the key takeaways instead of reading the whole thing
- **Problem Solving**: Talk through a work challenge, explore options, or ask for a different perspective on a decision you're stuck on

## Tips for better conversations

- **Start with context.** The more Gemini knows about your situation, the better the response. Instead of "write me an email," try "write me a follow-up email to my manager about the budget report I submitted last Friday — keep it short and professional."
- **Push back.** If the first response doesn't fit, say so. "That's too formal" or "Can you make it shorter?" works. AI gives better answers when you challenge it.
- **Use your Digital Persona.** If you completed the [Digital Persona challenge](https://weekend-ai-stuff.vercel.app/challenges/2026-03-14-digital-persona/), paste it at the start of a new chat. Gemini will already know your context before you ask your first question.
- **Try the RICO framework.** Structure your prompts with Role, Instructions, Context, and Output for more focused results. The [Prompt Engineering challenge](https://weekend-ai-stuff.vercel.app/challenges/2026-03-28-prompt-engineering/) walks you through it step by step.

## What makes it different from NotebookLM

Gemini is a general-purpose AI assistant. You can ask it about anything, and it pulls from its training and the web. NotebookLM is a research tool that only works with the documents you upload. Use Gemini when you need help thinking, writing, or getting things done. Use NotebookLM when you need to dig into specific material you already have.
