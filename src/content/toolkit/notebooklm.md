## Where it helps

- **Research**: Upload articles or reports and ask questions instead of reading everything end to end
- **Learning**: Turn your notes into quizzes, study guides, or mind maps
- **Productivity**: Drop in meeting notes, project docs, or long email threads and get quick summaries
- **Ideation**: Load multiple sources and ask NotebookLM to find connections or themes you might have missed

## What else it can do

- Generate a podcast-style audio summary you can listen to while commuting or doing something else
- Build a mind map that visually connects ideas across your documents
- Create a slide deck based on your sources
- Produce a video overview that turns your material into a short narrated walkthrough
- Quiz you with flashcards or multiple-choice questions based on what you uploaded
