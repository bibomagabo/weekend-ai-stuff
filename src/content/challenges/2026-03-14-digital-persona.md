Think of this like meeting a new officemate. Before they can help you with anything, they need to know a bit about who you are, what you do, and how you like to work. That's exactly what you're doing here, except your new officemate is AI.

By the end of this challenge, you'll have a short document called your Digital Persona. You can paste it into any AI tool to give it instant context about you before you even ask your first question.

---

## Step 1: Download Google Gemini

Search for "Google Gemini" on the App Store or Google Play. Sign in with your Google account. Done.

If you already have Gemini or prefer a different AI tool, that works too.

---

## Step 2: Open a new chat and paste this message

> *"Hi! I want you to help me create a short personal profile so you can assist me better. Please interview me — ask me one question at a time, and wait for my answer before asking the next one. Ask me about: my job and what I do, how I write and communicate, what I'm currently working on, how I like to work, and what bothers me or annoys me when talking to an AI. After 8 to 10 questions, write me a short summary under 300 words that I can use to introduce myself to any AI tool."*

---

## Step 3: Answer like you're telling a friend or relative about yourself

Gemini will ask you questions one by one. No need to overthink it — answer the way you would if a close friend or a cousin asked you about your work over dinner. The more honest and specific you are, the better your Digital Persona will turn out.

---

## Step 4: Give Gemini a name

This is optional — but it makes a difference. Try typing something like: *"By the way, I'm going to call you Jejomar."* Or Junel. Or Brando. Whatever feels natural.

Naming your AI makes the whole experience feel less like operating a machine and more like talking to someone. Very Filipino of us — we name everything.

---

## Step 5: Save your Digital Persona

Once Gemini writes your summary, copy it and save it somewhere easy to find — your Notes app, Google Docs, or Google Keep all work great.

This is your Digital Persona. Next time you open any AI tool, paste it at the start of the chat. The AI will already know your context, your preferences, and what you don't like — before you even ask your first question.

---

## You just learned three real things:

- How to introduce yourself to an AI — and why it matters
- That the more it knows about you, the more useful it becomes
- That building your Digital Persona takes less time than a lunch break
