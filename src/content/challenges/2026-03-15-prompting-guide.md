Start here if you're new to prompting — or if you've been winging it and want a better foundation.

**Google's 2026 Prompting Guide**
[Download the PDF](https://services.google.com/fh/files/misc/workspace_with_gemini_prompting_guide.pdf)

Quick, practical, and not just for Gemini. The techniques here apply to any AI platform you're already using.

---

## Try this today

1. Download the PDF and save it to your device
2. Open [Gemini](https://gemini.google.com) and upload the file
3. Paste this prompt and make it yours:

> *"I've uploaded Google's 2026 Prompting Guide. Based on the techniques in this guide, help me write a better prompt for something I'm working on right now: {your task or topic}. Walk me through what makes the prompt effective."*

{your task or topic} — describe what you're working on in one or two sentences. Examples: *summarizing a long email thread*, *preparing for a performance review*, *explaining a report to my manager*

Use AI to learn AI. That's the move.
